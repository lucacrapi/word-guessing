      How to Play Word Cue

      🎯 Objective — Guess the hidden word. The game tells you how "hot" or "cold" your guess is using AI semantic similarity.

      📅 Game modes

      • Daily Challenge — one secret word per day, per language. Everyone gets the same word, and a new one appears at midnight UTC.
      • Practice — play as many random rounds as you like.

      🔥 How it works

      • Type a common word: a noun, adjective, or verb (person, place, thing, idea, quality, or action)
      • Get a similarity score from 0–100
      • Higher scores mean your guess is closer in meaning to the secret word
      • Use hints if you get stuck

      📊 Score scale

      • 90–100: Very Hot! Almost there
      • 75–89: Hot! Getting closer
      • 50–74: Warm — right direction
      • 25–49: Cool — not quite
      • 0–24: Cold — way off!
